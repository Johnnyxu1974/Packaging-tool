🔧 Product Properties · 产品属性
Fragility classification guide / 易碎性分类说明
• Non-fragile: plastic, metal, or other products not normally expected to crack, shatter, or leak during distribution.
非易碎：如塑料、金属等，在常规运输中通常不会破裂、碎裂或泄漏。
• Fragile (glass): products containing glass parts or glass-dominant breakable components.
易碎（玻璃）：含玻璃部件或以玻璃为主要易碎材料的产品。
• Fragile (no glass): products without glass but with other breakable materials, such as stone, ceramic, porcelain, clay, or similar components that may crack or chip during distribution.
易碎（非玻璃）：不含玻璃，但含石材、陶瓷、瓷器、黏土类或类似易碎部件，运输中可能开裂或崩边。
• Amazon note: rigid fragile products under 50 lb generally require ISTA 6-Amazon Type A certified-lab testing, while rigid non-fragile products may use Amazon self-drop or ISTA 6-Amazon Type A.
Amazon说明：50 lb 以下刚性易碎产品通常需采用认证实验室执行 ISTA 6-Amazon Type A；刚性非易碎产品通常可采用 Amazon self-drop 或 ISTA 6-Amazon Type A。

Amazon Current SIPP Packaging Requirements (Reference)
Amazon 当前 SIPP 包装要求（参考说明）

1. Minimum pack size for SIPP eligibility / 1. SIPP 合格的最小包装尺寸
  • Minimum pack dimensions: 6.0 × 4.0 × 0.375 in
  • Packs below this size are not eligible for SIPP certification, because they cannot accommodate the standard 6 × 4 in shipping label.
  • 最小包装尺寸要求：6.0 × 4.0 × 0.375 英寸。
  • 低于该尺寸的包装通常不符合 SIPP 认证要求，因为无法容纳标准 6 × 4 英寸运单标签。
2. Rigid-pack structural requirements / 2. 刚性包装结构要求
  • Rigid packaging should be six-sided.
  • No protrusions, windows, or cut-outs.
  • Hand holes are allowed only within Amazon-defined limits.
  • Packaging should be properly sealed and designed to avoid unnecessary handling risk.
  • 刚性包装应为六面体结构。
  • 不应有突出物、开窗或挖孔。
  • 提手孔仅可在 Amazon 允许范围内设置。
  • 包装应充分封合，并尽量避免额外操作和搬运风险。
3. Flexible packaging / slack-fill requirement / 3. 柔性包装 / 空隙量要求
  • Flexible packaging should be right-sized and should not contain excess slack-fill.
  • When lifted at one corner, cumulative slack-fill should not exceed 2 in in any direction.
  • This slack-fill requirement applies mainly to flexible packaging, not rigid packs.
  • 柔性包装应尽量贴合产品尺寸，不应存在过量空隙。
  • 当拎起包装袋一个角时，任一方向的累计空隙量不应超过 2 英寸。
  • 该空隙量要求主要适用于柔性包装，并不直接等同于刚性包装的孔隙率要求。
4. Note / 4. 说明
  • This section is provided as a fixed reference for key Amazon SIPP requirements.
  • For detailed certification rules, always refer to the latest official Amazon SIPP guideline.
  • 本部分作为 Amazon SIPP 关键要求的固定参考说明。
  • 具体认证判定仍应以最新 Amazon 官方 SIPP 指南为准。
